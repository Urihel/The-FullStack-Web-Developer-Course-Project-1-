Gran Turismo Sport

Welcome to the future of motorsports - the definitive motor racing experience is back and better than ever only on PlayStation 4. Available in 4K HDR Wide Color at 60 FPS with the PS4 PRO, Gran Turismo Sport is the world's first racing experience to be built from the ground up to bring global, online competitions sanctioned by the highest governing body of international motorsports, the FIA (Federation International Automobile). Create your legacy as you represent and compete for your home country or favorite manufacturer.

A Major Evolution in Racing

  • Introducing the world's first and only online championships certified by the FIA (Federation International Automobile) - Represent and compete for your home country or favorite car manufacturer through two sanctioned Championships: Nations Cup & Manufacturers Fan Cup
  • Advanced Matchmaking - Compete against your friends or the world with multiple class divisions across age, region, and driving manners, allow drivers of all skill levels to race and develop individually
  • Driver Identity - Create your own legacy as you create, manage, and compare your professional racing career against drivers around the world through numerous events, challenges, and seasonal race leagues
  • Watch & Participate - Compete in races or watch the best in the world go head to head in a live race program as they compete to build their own driving legacy, and to be crowned global champions.

The Real Racing Simulator

  • Real world physics and vehicle dynamics - Offering the most realistic driving experiences and guidance for drivers of all skill levels
  • Race from over 140 Super Premium Cars - Coveted high-performance cars active in real world motorsports, each painstakingly remodeled from scratch to unprecedented quality and with the highest exterior and interior detail possible on any gaming platform
  • Legendary and GT Historic Race Circuits - Race your way through 27 different tracks across 19 locations from around the world, including the ever-popular Nürburgring, the legendary Tokyo Expressway, and the newly added half mile oval track Northern Isle Speedway

The Ultimate Gran Turismo Experience

  • Race Offline - Build your personalized garage and compete in an endless array of challenges in Campaign Mode or further improve your skills through Arcade (Single Race, Time Trial, Drift Trial, and 2- Player Split Screen)
  • Vision GT - More than two dozen exclusive concept cars, designed exclusively with auto manufacturers and global brands, including Bugatti, Aston Martin, BMW and Mercedes
  • Brand Central - A new form of car discovery through an interactive past, present, and future timeline through the lens of auto manufacturers
  • Livery Customization - With a fully featured livery editor, choose and personalize your vehicle with your favorite performance brand and logos
  • Photo Scapes - Featuring stunning real world images from across the globe, place your cars anywhere to create custom images shareable on social media
  • PlayStation® VR Compatible (Select Tracks in PS VR mode) - Experience the future as you get behind the wheel of 140 super premium cars with PS VR
